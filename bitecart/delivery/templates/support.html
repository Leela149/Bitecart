💡 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing them. Go to your active cart or contact support directly for immediate cancellation assistance.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes between 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the preparation time of the restaurant and your distance from the venue.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Address changes are supported before food preparation starts. Please contact support immediately with your new details to adjust the routing.
